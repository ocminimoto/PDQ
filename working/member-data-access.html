Membership Data Access

Instructions

The Member information on our website is provided on a need to know basis.  If your duties require you to have this information, contact the President for authorization and he will give you the password to allow access. 

"All Data" should be checked to see all members in our data base, current, passed, and new members in process.  If this is checked the data will be sorted by membership year and whatever is checked in the Secondary Sort Preference.  If it is not checked, only current and past year members will be included in the report.

The password is changed every January 1st.
