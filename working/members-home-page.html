Security

Members information in this section has been protected in a number of ways:

  1. a password is required to get here;
  2. web pages have been given un-guessable names;
  3. this complete section is labeled "off limits" to all legitimate search engines (see explanation below); and
  4. all email addresses have been concealed from unethical search engines looking for addresses for spamming purposes.

Please be aware that these security measures are not sufficient to stop a serious, knowledgeable intruder. On the other hand, it is highly unlikely that such a person would be interested in spending much effort to obtain information that has little or no financial value.

Members' Site contents

Included in this section are:

- Member photos, so that when we know a face but forgot the name, we can check. This will be especially useful for new members, as most of us have problems remembering a lot of new names and faces.

- Trainer information.

- A Member Page for each member which can contain (at the discretion of the member):

  1. contact information,
  2. a hobby focused biography (presence indicated by a "b" after their name in the menu),
  3. a few pictures of them and their aircraft (presence indicated by an "s" after their name),
  4. a link to their Photobucket account if they have one (presence indicated by a "p" after their name),
  5. a link to a personal website if they have one (presence indicated by a "w" after their name), and
  6. a comments section for the member to alert the webmaster of errors or ommisions on their page.

Procedures for managing member information

If any information on your member page is incorrect, please add a comment on your page using the Comment facility. That alerts the webmaster who will make the necessary change and send you an email when it is done.

If you think that information on another member's page is incorrect, please send an email to the webmaster.

In the event that you do not want some or any of your information on the website, please send an email to the webmaster advising what information is to be excluded.

Procedures for adding photographs to the Member Page

Each member can include up to 10 photographs on their member page. Changes can be made anytime, although we respectfully request that members be aware that the webmaster has to spend some amount of time making the changes, so frequent changes could be a burden.

The mechanism you should use to add member photos to your page is Photobucket. This photo management tool is described in Photo Central. One of the albums you set up should be "MemberPics" an it should be shared (default) so the webmaster can retrieve the pictures. As soon as ;you have uploaded your photos to this album, add a comment on your Member Page asking for them to be added. Include your logon name in Photobucket and the webmaster will put them in your member slide show.

If this procedure is too daunting for you (even with the help tutorial), just send them to me via email.
